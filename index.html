Privacy Policy for Subtopic

Effective Date: December 7th, 2024

We at Subtopic are strongly committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe environment for users of all ages. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

When using Subtopic, we collect the following types of information:

a. Personal Information

Google Sign-In Data: When signing in with Google, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Name
  • Profile picture (if available)
  • Other account details provided by Google during sign-in process

Device ID: We collect and store the device ID to ensure single-device access per account.

b. Chat Data

All user interactions, such as questions and AI-generated responses, are stored in cloud storage to allow access on any logged-in device.

c. Purchase Data

Details of in-app purchases, such as transaction IDs and credits purchased, but not sensitive payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Authentication

To manage secure access to the app and restrict usage to one device per account.

Educational Content Delivery

To provide AI-generated responses strictly relevant to the selected subtopics, ensuring safe and age-appropriate content.

Personalization

To store and retrieve chat histories for seamless user experience across devices.

Service Operation

To facilitate AI responses through trusted API providers.

Payment Management

To process in-app purchases and manage credits for extended app usage.

3. Sharing Your Information

We respect your privacy and only share data in limited scenarios:

Third-Party API Providers

We collaborate with AI service providers like Gemini 1.5 Flash-8B, Claude-Haiku, Nvidia Nemotron, and Mistral to generate responses. Limited interaction data may be shared strictly for query processing.

Service Providers

Trusted third-party vendors handle cloud storage and payment processing securely.

Legal Obligations

Data may be disclosed to comply with legal obligations or protect our legal rights.

4. Child Privacy and Safety

Subtopic is designed for users aged 13 and above. We are committed to providing a safe and age-appropriate experience for young users by ensuring the following:

Age-Appropriate Content

AI-generated content is strictly related to educational subtopics and is carefully curated to avoid inappropriate or irrelevant responses.

Thorough Prompt Review

While we rely on third-party API providers (e.g., Gemini Flash 3B, Claude-Haiku, Nvidia Nemotron, and Mistral) to generate AI responses, all prompts are thoroughly reviewed and rectified within our system before being sent to these providers. This ensures the responses remain relevant, appropriate, and suitable for young users.

No Direct Data Collection from Children

We do not knowingly collect personal data from users under 13. If we become aware of such data being collected inadvertently, it will be promptly deleted.

Parental or Guardian Consent

If you are between 13 and 18 years of age, you must obtain consent from a parent or guardian before using our application. By using Subtopic, you confirm that you have received such consent.

5. Third-Party AI Providers

To deliver AI-generated responses, Subtopic integrates with trusted third-party API providers, including but not limited to Gemini Flash-1.5 8B, Claude-Haiku, Nvidia Nemotron, and Mistral. These providers process user queries and context to generate responses relevant to the selected subtopics.

We only share the minimum necessary data required for these services, such as the text of user queries and contextual information. We do not share personally identifiable information (e.g., email addresses or device IDs) with these providers.

Each provider processes data under their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review their terms:

  • Gemini Flash 3B: Privacy Policy
  • Claude-Haiku: Privacy Policy
  • Nvidia Nemotron: Privacy Policy
  • Mistral: Privacy Policy

By using Subtopic, you acknowledge that your data may be processed by these providers to enable app functionality.

6. Data Storage and Retention

  • Personal Information: Stored securely for the duration of your account activity.
  • Chat Data: Retained in cloud storage for retrieval on any logged-in device.
  • Device ID: Stored for single-device account management.

7. Data Security

We employ industry-standard measures to safeguard your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for data in transit and at rest.
  • Secure storage and regular audits to prevent unauthorized access.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update any inaccuracies in your information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Portability: Obtain a copy of your data in a portable format.

To exercise your rights, contact us at subtopic.in@gmail.com
